Mohammed Saeed

As of October 2019, I am a PhD student in the Data Science Department at Eurecom, under the supervision of Prof. Paolo Papotti. My research focuses on using Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning to automate fact-checking. Specifically, I'm interested in fact-checking in natural language using structured data.
Since the beginning of the COVID pandemic, we have adapted one of our fact-checking systems to mitigate fake coronavirus new, and launched it publicly. CoronaCheck is being supported by a Google and an IFCN/Youtube grant.
In my spare time, I enjoy fiddling and strumming my uke.
